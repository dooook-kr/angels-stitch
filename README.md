# Angel's Stitch: Antigravity Pattern Maker

English | [Français](README_FR.md) | [한국어](README_KR.md)

## 📌 Introduction
Angel's Stitch is a cross-stitch pattern generator and online work management app created for my French mother who loves cross-stitching. It allows you to easily turn your favorite photos into patterns for download, and track your stitching progress on a smartphone or tablet without the need for paper patterns.

The name "Angel's Stitch" was inspired by my mother's name, Angelique. It carries my heartfelt wish that her precious hobby time becomes even more joyful and peaceful through this app.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Patterns from Photos:** Automatically convert photos into cross-stitch patterns with image upload, aspect ratio cropping, and color quantization.
* **Digital Patterns:** View patterns in real-time on the web and conveniently track your progress by touching completed stitches.
* **High-Resolution PDF Downloads:** Supports downloading crisp, high-quality PDF patterns for printing or archiving.
* **3-Step View Mode:** Switch freely between 'Split View', 'Full Pattern View', and 'Full Thread Info' for maximum convenience.
* **Trilingual Support:** Fully supports English, French, and Korean for easy use anywhere in the world.
* **Mobile Optimization & Gestures:** Intuitively control patterns with mobile-optimized screens and finger gestures (pinch-to-zoom, pan).
